> *"The best platforms are built by teams that trust each other. My job is to create the conditions — clarity, safety, and momentum — that make great engineering possible."*

**Build for Durability, Not the Sprint**  
I have a strong bias toward durable foundations over short-term velocity. Every platform I've launched — MAPLE, CAMP, UMR, Data Acceleration — was designed to be adopted beyond its original use case. Platforms should multiply team leverage, not create dependencies.

Translating LLMs and agentic AI from experiment to enterprise-grade production requires judgment, not just capability. I've done it — and I build teams that can do it repeatedly.

**Scale the Humans, Not Just the Systems**  
I've grown orgs from 12 to 25+ engineers without sacrificing quality or culture. The most important architecture decisions I make are about team structure, hiring bars, and how we make decisions together.
